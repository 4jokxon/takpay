# TakPay

TakPay is a stablecoin payment link and invoice platform built on Arc Testnet.

Merchants can create invoices, share a payment link, receive USDC payments, verify transactions on-chain, and generate receipts. TakPay also includes a payment intelligence layer for fraud checks, refund decisions, routing recommendations, and settlement workflows.

## What TakPay does

- Creates stablecoin invoices and payment links
- Lets customers pay with USDC on Arc
- Verifies payment transactions from the blockchain
- Tracks invoice states: `pending`, `submitted`, `paid`, and `expired`
- Generates receipts for confirmed payments
- Sends merchant webhooks on payment events
- Uses an agent layer for payment intelligence
- Integrates Circle Developer-Controlled Wallets for settlement

## Why it matters

Stablecoin payments are powerful, but raw wallet transfers are not enough for merchant workflows. A business needs clear invoices, payment state, receipts, webhook events, fraud checks, and settlement logic.

TakPay turns an on-chain USDC transfer into a structured checkout experience.

## One-liner

TakPay is an agentic stablecoin payment link and invoice platform built on Arc, powered by on-chain verification, Circle wallet settlement, and payment intelligence.
