# Fraud detection

The fraud engine analyzes invoices and payment context before finalizing a payment.

## Example checks

- New wallet risk
- High amount risk
- Velocity risk
- Self-payment patterns
- Suspicious round-number behavior

## Risk handling

- Low risk: approve
- Medium risk: approve or flag depending on rule mix
- High risk: flag for review
- Critical risk: reject or block

TakPay can block critical-risk payments during verification while still allowing normal low-risk payments to settle.
